FAQs

 Why we do FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

Our country is currently facing an upward surge of non-communicable diseases such as diabetes and cardiovascular diseases. It is due as the number of people with obesity increases. This is supported by a study published in the British Medical Journal, The Lancet showed that Malaysia is the most obese country in Asia. In one study done by the Ministry of Health Malaysia, National Health & Morbidity Survey (NHMS) 2015 showed that nearly half of the Malaysian population aged 18 and above (47.7%) were overweight or obese. What's more, the national prevalence of obesity had increased by 2.6% respectively as compared to the previous findings of NHMS 2011.

This ALARMING trend of obesity epidemic in Malaysia does require an IMMEDIATE ACTION.

 How FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP helps?

FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP is simply a platform for the urban people to work towards practicing healthier lifestyle.

We are a team of experienced Fitness Coaches, Mindfulness Coach, Nutritionist, and Medical Officer.

With the ideal combination of 8 weeks of real workouts from the Fitness Coaches, the positive mindset lessons from the Mindfulness Coach, the right diet advices from the Nutritionist, and the health consultation from the Medical Officer expect SUCCESSFUL result. We GUARANTEE it.

 What is FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP is a body transformation program targeted to a number of 35 obese people in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. People who are -  READY TO START, READY TO GO BEYOND, READY TO TRANSFORM, READY TO BE GREATER.

There are two (2) phases. Firstly, CONTEST PHASE. Secondly, MAINTENANCE PHASE.

1. CONTEST PHASE is an eight (8) weeks transformation contest of BOOTCAMP Group Classes and Personal Training sessions with a total winning cash prizes of RM14,000.00.
2. MAINTENANCE PHASE is a phase designed for the continuation of the CONTEST PHASE. It is a sixteen (16) weeks supportive program with a once per week of Personal Training session. It is an OPTIONAL phase.

How to participate FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

1. Register as a contestant by choosing our SILVER PLAN, GOLD PLAN or PLATINUM PLAN before June 9, 2018 (Payment only be made from June 10, 2018 till June 30, 2018).
2. Contestant must be aged 18 years and above regardless of gender, race and nationality.
3. Contestant currently lived, studying and/or working in Kuala Lumpur or Selangor area.
4. Contestant has a Body Mass Index (BMI) of at least more than 27.50 (Class Obese I) before June 9, 2018.

BMI Calculation = weight (kg) / (height X height) (m²)
Example: 80.0kg / (1.50m X 1.50m) = 35.56

Malaysian Clinical Practice Guidelines of Obesity (2004) BMI Classes (kg/m²):
Underweight <18.50 | Normal 18.50-22.99 | Overweight 23.00-27.49
Obese I 27.50-34.99 | Obese II 35.00-39.9 | Obese III >40.00

 How to win FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

Upon completion of the eight (8) weeks transformation contest, the winners of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP will be selected based on the percentage improvement of the health parameters in the Health Screening done (pre-intervention and post-intervention). So, it is not just merely in losing weight. It is more in getting FIT. Thus, this is why the program is called as FAT2FIT.

 What is BOOTCAMP Workouts?

BOOTCAMP workouts are designed to improve an overall fitness inclusive of strength, cardio, flexibility, agility and endurance for any ages! Never in each of the sessions will be the same. If you are someone who get bored in a gym and find it hard to develop a habit of exercise, then BOOTCAMP workouts are the RIGHT CHOICE.

An advantage of a BOOTCAMP workouts is that a group dynamic will often help motivate each others. People make friends and socialize as they exercise.
